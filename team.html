Who are we at OceanScope?

We are a group of six dedicated students studying Industrial Economics and Management at Chalmers University. As part of our academic journey, we recently embarked on a course titled "Leading in a Digital World," which has provided us with valuable insights into leveraging digital technologies for positive impact. Passionate about environmental sustainability, we have taken up the challenge of filling the data gap in oceanic research. Through the utilization of advanced sensors, our website plays a vital role in collecting and analyzing ocean data.

By harnessing the power of technology, we aim to contribute to a better understanding of our oceans and promote initiatives for their conservation.
